Project summary

Inventory management and employee performance tracking software for physical stores.

Project details

Features

  1. Real-Time Inventory Tracking: Monitor your stock levels in real time and receive automatic notifications for products falling to critical levels.
  2. Employee Performance Analysis: Measure employee productivity with detailed reports based on sales targets, customer interaction, and working hours.
  3. Fast Stock Entry and Barcode Support: Add new products to the system in seconds and reduce the margin of error to zero by digitizing physical counting processes.
  4. In-Store Task Management: Assign daily or weekly tasks to your employees and remotely monitor the completion status of operational processes.
  5. Advanced Sales Reporting: Analyze best-selling products and peak hours to make strategic decisions that will increase your turnover.
  6. Cloud-Based Access: Securely access all inventory and personnel data from any device, even when you're not in the store.
